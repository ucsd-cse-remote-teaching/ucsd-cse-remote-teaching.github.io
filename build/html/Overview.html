Overview¶

Hello!

This Handbook was written by Ben Du and Sabeel Mansuri with the guidance of Joe Politz, Niema Moshiri, and Patrick Mallon. Its purpose is to aid instructors at UC San Diego with implementing remote courses.

The Handbook is a living document that is intended to cater to your needs! If you have any issues with remote instruction that we haven’t addressed yet, please reach out to anyone listed above.
